First lets prepare the ramen noodles. so to do that,

step one : Take a boiling pan and add about 400ml of water and let it boil

step two: Take a small bowl and add 2 tsp of sesame oil and 2 tsp of soy sauce 1 tsp of chilli oil and the ramen flavour packets.

step three: Once the water is boiling add the ramen to the water and let it cook

step four: take out the cooked ramen and run it under cold water for a few seconds and add them to a large bowl and simply pour the sauce we just made.

Step five: Finally, chop up some spring onions and fry an egg to ur liking and add on top of your ramen!
